Our Cooperating Teams

We work with local health workers, schools, community leaders, and volunteers to fight mosquito-borne diseases. These teams help us reach families and communities with the information and resources they need.

  • Local doctors and nurses provide health education and monitor outbreaks
  • Community leaders organize awareness campaigns and workshops
  • Volunteers distribute mosquito nets, repellents, and hygiene kits

Our Approach to Preventing Diseases

Our goal is to reduce the spread of diseases like malaria and dengue by providing practical help and education. We focus on prevention, treatment, and community awareness.

  • Providing mosquito nets and repellents to families in affected areas
  • Teaching children and adults how to prevent mosquito breeding
  • Running workshops to demonstrate safe practices and hygiene
  • Supporting local health programs with resources and guidance

Our Presence Around the World

Our organization is active in several countries where mosquito-borne diseases are most common. We collaborate with local partners to bring education, prevention tools, and support to communities in need.

  • Bangladesh
  • India
  • Nepal
  • Kenya
  • Tanzania
  • Philippines
  • Indonesia
