Volunteer with Us

Join our team of dedicated volunteers and help make a difference in communities affected by mosquito-borne diseases. You can participate in local campaigns, educational workshops, and prevention projects. Every contribution counts, no matter how small!

  • Assist in organizing awareness campaigns and events
  • Help distribute mosquito nets and repellents
  • Support educational workshops for youth and families

Our Cooperating Regions

We work closely with regions that are most affected by mosquito-borne diseases. Through partnerships with local organizations, schools, and community centers, we aim to provide education, resources, and practical support to prevent the spread of illness.

  • Partnering with local schools to teach children prevention methods
  • Supporting community health programs and campaigns
  • Providing resources such as mosquito nets, educational materials, and hygiene kits
